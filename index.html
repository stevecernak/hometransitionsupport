What I help with

Focused, non-emergency support for situations that disrupt comfort, independence, or confidence in the home — especially for seniors and families helping from a distance.

Home basics & walk-throughs

  • Doors, hardware, and small adjustments
  • Minor mounting and setup
  • Safety and aging-in-place walk-throughs
  • “Something feels off” assessments

Environmental comfort & moisture

  • Damp or musty rooms and basements
  • Short-term dehumidifier placement and rental
  • Seasonal humidity comfort issues
  • Clear guidance on whether larger work is needed

Basic tech clarity

  • Wi-Fi and device setup
  • Printers and TVs
  • Simplifying confusing setups
  • Patient, in-person explanations
Not a fit: emergency services, mold remediation, medical care, major electrical/plumbing/HVAC work, or large construction projects.
